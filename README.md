# From Strategy to Dashboard: An End-to-End Business Intelligence Study

**Organization:** Monzo Bank  
**Author:** Siddik Shaik  

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence (BI) case study that demonstrates how organizations transform business strategy into actionable insights through data, analytics, and dashboards. The study integrates real-world business scenarios from **Monzo Bank**, **Morrisonsâ€“Amazon**, and **Alpen Hotel** to illustrate strategic, tactical, and operational decision-making.

## Objectives
- Understand different decision types (strategic, tactical, operational)
- Design a modern BI architecture aligned with business needs
- Identify and apply relevant BI metrics
- Propose BI strategies and value propositions
- Design an executive dashboard to support data-driven decisions

## Key Topics Covered
- Business strategy and decision types  
- Business Intelligence architecture and data sources  
- Structured and unstructured data usage  
- BI metrics (profitability, revenue, risk)  
- Value proposition analysis using analytics  
- BI strategy using agile and modern data platforms  
- Dashboard design principles and KPI selection  

## BI Architecture
- Cloud-based, scalable BI architecture
- Integration of internal and external data sources
- Support for real-time and batch analytics
- Use of unstructured data (support chats, reviews, logs)
- Self-service analytics and visualization tools

## Analytics Techniques Used
- Descriptive Analytics  
- Diagnostic Analytics  
- Predictive Analytics  
- Prescriptive Analytics  
- Statistical reasoning and KPI evaluation  

## Dashboard Design
- Executive-level dashboard for Monzo Bank
- Key KPIs include:
  - Active Users
  - Revenue
  - Customer Support Tickets
  - Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  - Fraud Rate
- Focus on clarity, usability, and decision support

## Tools & Technologies
- Business Intelligence frameworks
- Data & Analytics strategy
- Dashboard design (Excel-based prototype)
- Cloud BI concepts
- Agile BI principles

## Key Outcomes
- Demonstrates how BI supports strategic and operational decisions
- Highlights the importance of aligning analytics with business goals
- Shows how dashboards convert data into actionable insights

## Conclusion
This project illustrates how effective Business Intelligence bridges the gap between business strategy and execution. By combining modern BI architecture, analytics techniques, and thoughtful dashboard design, organizations can enable faster, smarter, and more informed decision-making.
